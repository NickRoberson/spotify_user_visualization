Inspiration

I love music, always have. It's in most of my fondest memories from when I was a child, and continues to be now that I am older and in college. When I got to Grinnell College, after graiduating from Geoerge Mason High School in Virginia, I knew that I wanted to incorporate music into my college experience somehow. Right now you are looking at the culmination of that goal. This spring (2017), after completing a data visualization course I decided that I wanted to make a tool that helps people discover new music. I chose spotify because, at the time, it is one of the largest music streaming services out there, plus it has a great API.
